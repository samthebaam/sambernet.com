About

I studied Mechanical Engineering at ETH Zurich and I currently live in Zurich, Switzerland and work for Zurich Soft Robotics, where we are bringing beautiful moving solar facades to life. I grew up in Lucerne, but have lived abroad both in England and China.

This blog is a place for short notes: what I'm building, what I'm learning, and what I'm trying to understand better. Expect writing on engineering, startups, decision-making, and the occasional detour into tools, materials, or process.
